About me

Hi there!
My name is Olena Datso, and I am a passionate Full Stack Developer with over 5 years of experience in graphic design. I thrive on creating visually stunning and user-friendly digital experiences that combine aesthetics with functionality. I specialize in modern web technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React, and I’m constantly exploring ways to improve my skills. While I used to focus solely on designing visuals, I’ve transitioned to coding interfaces to turn those designs into reality—an evolution I absolutely love. Fun fact: I once considered a career in illustration, but I realized I love crafting interactive user experiences even more. So much for becoming a famous artist! When I’m not coding or designing, you’ll probably find me enjoying a good book, exploring the beauty of Calgary, or diving into new challenges that inspire creativity. Let’s create something amazing together!
